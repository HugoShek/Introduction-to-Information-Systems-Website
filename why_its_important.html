Why is Cyber Security Important?

Cyber security is important because smartphones, computers and the internet are now such a fundamental part of modern life, that it's difficult to imagine how we'd function without them. Below are examples of information that can be stolen.

  1. Passwords
  2. Phone number
  3. Address
  4. Accounts

On the "how to stay secure" page it will tel you how you can protect the data listed above.
