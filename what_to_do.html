What do you do when you are Cyberbullied?

When you or someone else falls vicitm of cyberbullying you should follow the instructions below.

  1. Save any evidence e.g., screenshots and show an adult
  2. Block the person cyberbullying you
  3. Log off
  4. Talk to someone you trust about it

If you feel like it's all too much for you, you can call helplines, talk about it to parents and friends.
